I try to grow at least 3 new varieties of vegetables each summer season. This was my first season ever growing eggplants, and it was a challenge. Eggplants are very particular about the amount of water and sunlight they get each day. While I had quite a few early specimens wither or split, I think I've finally got my methods down pat. I also grew yard-long beans and bok choy for the first time this season, but my previous experience with beans and leafy greens made those easy to maintain and bountiful.
